About Us

Typically in Math Club you would go to club meeting and recieve a practice problem packet to work on while chatting with your friends. Additionally, once every month or two, we would travel to a competition. But with the COVID-19 Pandemic, it has taken away this experience.

Now we hold lectures in two different breakout rooms, where we cover content for both advanced students who tend to be more experienced in competitions, and for beginner students who don't have competition experience. Tournaments aren't gone though. Despite the COVID-19 Pandemic, most tournaments will still be held except it will be online this year.
